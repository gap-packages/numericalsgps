  [33X[0;0YA good semigroup [22XS[122X is a submonoid of [22XN^2[122X, with the following properties.[133X

  under  infimums, if [22XC_1[122X and [22XC_2[122X fulfill [22XC_i+N^nâŠ† S[122X, then [22XC_1âˆ§ C_2+N^nâŠ† S[122X. So
  there  is  a minimum [22XC[122X fulfilling [22XC+N^nâŠ† S[122X, which is called the conductor of
  [22XS[122X.[133X
